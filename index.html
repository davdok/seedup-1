What we do

Seed-Up is the first hacker house start-up incubator in France. There, a dozen programmers, designers and business developers live, work and share their knowledge. An inexhaustible source of new ideas, Seed-Up works both on its own projects and external IT missions. The variety of talents, skill sharing and open innovation galvanizes our team, pushing our projects to new heights.
